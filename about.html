About Us

“Pioneering Excellence in Design and Technology Education”


Cirvee Design Academy is more than just an educational institution ; it's a transformative journey

Our vision

Our vision is to establish Cirvee as a beacon of tech education in West Africa and beyond, known for cultivating tech savvies who lead and innovate. We envision a future where our graduates are at the forefront of technological advancements, driving positive change and solving critical challenges across industries. As the most engaging and supportive tech hub, Cirvee aims to inspire a diverse community of learners to reach their full potential, making significant contributions to the tech world and society at large. Through our unwavering commitment to excellence and inclusivity, we aspire to not just teach technology but to imbue our students with a lifelong passion for learning, innovation, and making a difference.

Our mission

Cirvee’s mission is to transform tech enthusiasts from novices into savvies, empowering them with comprehensive, cutting-edge knowledge and skills. By providing an innovative and supportive learning environment, we aim to demystify technology and make it accessible to everyone, regardless of their starting point. Our focus is on hands-on, practical learning, supplemented by mentorship and community engagement, ensuring each student can navigate and excel in the ever-evolving tech landscape. We are committed to fostering a culture of continuous learning, creativity, and problem-solving, preparing our students to become the next generation of tech leaders and innovators.
